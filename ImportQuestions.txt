semantic vs non semantic
    Definition: Tags that clearly describe their meaning and content.

    Purpose: Improve accessibility, SEO, and code readability.

    Examples:

    <header> – Represents the page or section header

    <article> – Represents a self-contained composition

    <nav> – Represents navigation links

    <footer> – Represents the footer of a document or section


    Non-Semantic HTML:
    Definition: Tags that do not provide information about the content.

    Purpose: Used mainly for layout or styling without conveying content meaning.

    Examples:

    <div> – Generic container

    <span> – Generic inline container
